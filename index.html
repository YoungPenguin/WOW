Introduction

This website will take you on a tour, analysing the Major characters in Warcraft, by accessing their wowpedia pages and extracting user comments from their wowhead threads.

In order to do so, a network is created, where nodes are each of the major characters, and edges between them arise when one characters wowpedia page links to anothers wowpedia page. This resulted in a network consisting in 261 characters with 4006 edges connecting them, as seen on the visualization on the right.

Network Visualization

The nodes are colored according to their faction (red for Horde, blue for Alliance and gray for others, such as bosses in the game). Additionally, edges are colored according to the relationships of the two nodes, where gray links from any node to a neutral node, blue is between two alliance nodes, red is between two horde nodes and green is between a horde and alliance node.

Word Clouds

A way of getting the words that differentiate characters or groups of characters from each other is by creating wordclouds from TF-IDF scores. This has been done for different groupings of the Warcraft characters, which can be investigated using the dropdown menus below. You can see which words describes your favorite group of characters, whether it be Horde or Alliance, and even which words other players use to describe said characters!
